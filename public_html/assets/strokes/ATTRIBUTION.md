Stroke order diagrams in this directory and in `../strokes-animated/` are derived from the
[Make Me a Hanzi](https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi) project (Shaunak Kishore), which
itself derives its glyph/stroke data from two Arphic Technology fonts released under the
Arphic Public License. Files are recolored from the project's defaults to match this
workspace's palette; stroke shapes, order, and numbering are unmodified.

Source data: https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi (graphics.txt, svgs-still/, svgs/)
